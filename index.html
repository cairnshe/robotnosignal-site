What is Robot No Signal?

Robot No Signal is an online platform built for children and teenagers (ages 6–18) to share, trade, and sell their original digital artwork and music. We believe every child deserves a place to be seen, heard, and celebrated.

With parent-managed accounts and safety-first design, Robot No Signal empowers kids to take control of their creative journey while staying protected. We are building not just a platform — but a movement.

How It Works

  • Create an account with a guardian's help
  • Upload original artwork or music
  • Sell or barter your creations
  • Get recognized, get rewarded, and stay safe

Platform Features

  • Original content only — protected with watermarking
  • Refundable CAD $50 seller deposit
  • CAD $9.9/month membership — no commissions or listing fees
  • Optional barter system
  • Community badges and creator challenges
  • Guardian-managed accounts for child safety
